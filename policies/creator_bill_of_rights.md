# Barta Creator Bill of Rights
Version 1.0
Effective: March 04, 2026

___

## Introduction

The Barta Creator Bill of Rights defines the protections creators have when selling on Barta.

These commitments guide how the marketplace operates and how policies evolve over time.
They are designed to ensure creators can build businesses on Barta with confidence, transparency, and predictable platform rules.

This document is not a legal contract. The Barta Terms of Service remain the governing legal agreement.
Instead, the Creator Bill of Rights describes how Barta commits to operating the platform.

___

# 1. Right to Stable Platform Rules

Creators should not be surprised by silent platform rule changes that affect their businesses.

Barta commits to:

- Publishing meaningful policy changes publicly
- Maintaining visible version history for policies affecting creators
- Recording important policy versions in the Barta transparency ledger

Creators must be able to see:

- when a rule changed
- what version of a rule is currently active
- previous versions of the rule

___

# 2. Right to Transparent Visibility

Creators deserve clarity about how their listings and shops are treated on the platform.

Barta commits to:

- clearly labeling sponsored or promoted placements
- avoiding hidden suppression of listings without explanation
- providing understandable reasons when visibility is restricted due to policy enforcement

The goal is to ensure creators do not feel like marketplace behavior is unpredictable or opaque.

___

# 3. Right to Clear Moderation and Enforcement

Platform enforcement actions should be understandable and reviewable.

When a listing, shop, or account is affected by enforcement actions, Barta will provide:

- the reason for the action
- the policy or rule applied
- guidance on how to resolve the issue when possible

Where appropriate, creators may have the ability to appeal enforcement decisions.

___

# 4. Right to Data Portability

Creators should retain access to the data they generate through their work.

Barta commits to providing creators the ability to export relevant business data, including:

- listings
- order history
- reviews
- shop information

This ensures creators maintain control over their business data and are not locked into the platform.

___

# 5. Right to Honest Promotions

Promotions and advertising should be clearly identifiable.

Barta commits that:

- paid promotions may influence listing visibility, but they will always be clearly labeled

This ensures buyers and creators can trust the integrity of the marketplace.

___

# 6. Right to Public Platform Commitments

Barta believes platform commitments should be transparent and verifiable.

To support this, Barta maintains:

- a public Trust Center describing platform policies and commitments
- visible policy version history
- a transparency ledger that records important policy attestations

These mechanisms allow creators and the public to verify that commitments remain consistent over time.

___

# Versioning and Evolution

The Creator Bill of Rights may evolve as Barta grows.

When updates occur:

- new versions will be published publicly
- previous versions will remain visible
- significant updates may be recorded in the transparency ledger

Example version structure:

```
Version 1.0 - Initial Release
Version 1.1 - Clarifications or expanded protections
```

___

# Relationship to the Trust Center

The Creator Bill of Rights is part of Barta's broader transparency framework.

Related resources may include:

- Trust Center
- Policy Archive
- Transparency Ledger
- Consumer Protections

These systems help ensure Barta's commitments remain visible, consistent, and verifiable.

___

# Final Note

The Creator Bill of Rights reflects Barta's commitment to building a marketplace that respects creators as partners.

Creators should be able to build businesses on the platform knowing that:

- rules will be visible
- policies will not change silently
- enforcement will be explainable
- their work and data will remain under their control

These principles guide how Barta operates today and how it will evolve in the future.
