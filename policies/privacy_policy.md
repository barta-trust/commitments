# 1. Information We Collect

## 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**  
When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, date of birth, and password.

**Profile Information:**  
Display name, profile picture, bio, and platform interests you choose to share.

**Seller Information:**  
If you register as a seller, we collect your shop name, shop description, location (city, state, country), avatar and banner images, SKUs, product listings, and pricing information.

**Payment Information:**  
When you connect a Stripe account for selling, Stripe collects your bank account details, tax identification number, and identity verification documents directly. Barta does not store your full bank account or tax ID numbers. These are held by Stripe.

**Communications:**  
Messages you send to other users through our messaging system, and any communications you send to our support team.

**Phone Number:**  
When you register or log in using phone-based authentication, we collect your mobile phone number. We use it to send you one-time verification codes (OTP) via SMS. Your phone number is shared with our SMS delivery provider (Vonage) solely for the purpose of delivering these verification messages.

**Imported Data:**  
If you use our CSV import feature (e.g., importing listings from Etsy), we process the file contents to create listings on your behalf.

---

## 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device and Browser Information:**  
IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.

**Usage Data:**  
Pages visited, features used, time spent on pages, click patterns, and referring URLs.

**Authentication Data:**  
Session tokens, login timestamps, and authentication method used.

**Performance Data:**  
Page load times and error logs to maintain and improve the Service.

---

## 1.3 Information from Third Parties

**Stripe:**  
We receive confirmation of your payment account status (e.g., whether your Stripe account is fully onboarded and charges-enabled). We do not receive your full bank details from Stripe.

**Invite Links:**  
If you join Barta via an invitation link, we associate your account with the inviting user for referral tracking.

# 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide and operate the Service.** Create and maintain your account, process listings, facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers, and enable messaging.
- **Process payments.** Connect your Stripe account, facilitate payouts to sellers, and process buyer purchases.
- **Communicate with you.** Send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates, message notifications) and, with your consent, marketing emails (newsletters, promotions, new features).
- **Improve and personalize the Service.** Analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, develop new features, and personalize your experience.
- **Ensure safety and security.** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access; enforce our Terms of Service; and comply with legal obligations.
- **Fulfill seller onboarding.** Guide sellers through shop setup, Stripe Connect integration, listing creation, and shop publication.
- **Provide customer support.** Respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and process your data rights requests.

# 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only in the following circumstances:

**Between Buyers and Sellers**  
When a transaction occurs, we share necessary information between the buyer and seller to fulfill the order (e.g., shipping address, buyer name). Seller shop profiles, including shop name, bio, location, and listings, are publicly visible.

**Service Providers**  
We share information with third-party service providers who process data on our behalf.

**Legal Requirements**
We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

**Business Transfers**
If Barta is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice on the Service before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

**With Your Consent**  
We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

# 7. Advertising Attribution and Campaign Measurement

To understand whether our marketing campaigns are effective, Barta may collect limited conversion events such as:

- App installs
- Account registrations
- Purchases or completed transactions

These events may be transmitted to advertising platforms through secure server-to-server integrations. This information is used solely to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and optimize how we reach potential creators and buyers.

**Why we disclose this.** Most platforms transmit conversion data to advertising networks without mentioning it in their privacy policy, or bury it in vague language about "business partners." We believe you should know exactly what data leaves our systems and why.

**What we do not share.** We do not provide advertising platforms with browsing histories, marketplace activity, or behavioral profiles. We do not sell personal data to advertisers. Only minimal conversion signals (e.g., "a purchase occurred") are transmitted, without details about what was purchased, from whom, or other marketplace context.

**Our commitment.** Barta does not share your marketplace activity, purchase history, or selling data with external advertising platforms for the purpose of ad targeting. Your marketplace data stays within Barta.

As Barta grows, we intend to increasingly rely on internal measurement and first-party analytics so that we can reduce our reliance on external advertising platforms over time.

# 8. Data Retention

**Account Data:** Retained while your account remains active. Deleted or anonymized within 30 days of account deletion unless legally required otherwise.

**Transaction Records:** Retained for at least 7 years to comply with tax and financial regulations.

**Messages:** Stored while both users maintain accounts. Deleted accounts will have messages anonymized.

**Seller Data:** Retained while seller accounts remain active. Archived listings are retained for 12 months before permanent deletion.

**Server Logs:** Retained up to 90 days for security and debugging.
